.deduplicatify_

Deduplicatify detects any duplicate songs in a Spotify playlist even if they are from different albums. The detection algorithm will label two songs as duplicates if their:

  • song names are similar
  • artists are the same
  • song length is within 3 seconds of each other.

The algorithm also does not label remixed, live and acoustic versions as a duplicate of their original counterpart.
